Welcome to ShadeStore

Your One-Stop Destination for Premium Paints and Home Decor Solutions

ShadeStore, established in 2010, is the online platform of Nellithanam Paints - a trusted name in the paint industry for over two decades. We provide high-quality paints, tools, and home decor solutions to customers across Kerala.

At ShadeStore, we believe that the right color can transform any space. Our mission is to help you find the perfect shade that resonates with your style and personality.

Our Vision

To be the leading online destination for all painting and home decoration needs, providing customers with premium products, exceptional service, and innovative solutions.

Our Values

  • Quality Excellence - We never compromise on the quality of our products.
  • Customer First - Your satisfaction is our top priority.
  • Innovation - We continuously evolve to meet changing customer needs.
  • Sustainability - We are committed to eco-friendly practices and products.
